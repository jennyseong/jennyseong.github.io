About Jenny

A little bit of my background...

Born and raised in Seoul, South Korea. Lived in England for a little while in my childhood. I graduated from Dartmouth College in 2016, majoring in Engineering Sciences and Studio Art. I'm currently living in Hanover, NH, enrolled in the Digital Arts masters program at Dartmouth College.
