Memoriscapes the Book

1st Place Artist Book – Dartmouth Book Arts Prize

Memoriscapes the Book

The book version of the sculptural project that shares its name. The book is made in four different parts, and the drawing on the pages are made so that whichever page you are on in whichever section of the book, the whole object creates a connected, complete picture. The book was bound using codex binding, with a single acrylic sheet as the back and four smaller laser-engraved sheets and the front cover for each section. The book won First Place in the Artist Book section of the Dartmouth Book Arts Awards in 2016, and is currently on display at Dartmouth's Baker-Berry Library in Hanover, NH.

  • Medium: Laser-engraved acrylic sheets and ink on paper
  • Year: 2016
