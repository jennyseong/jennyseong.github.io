Kodama and the Magic Seeds

Character Designer and Writer, 2016

A game made in Unity 3D and Maya 2016. Collaboration with Sean Oh and Armin Mahban.
Check out our website here.

The main menu features Kodama taking a nap with "Mother," the tree that is the heart of the forest.

We aimed to create a relaxing platformer with low-poly style models and slightly muted colors.
The most basic idea was that the setting would be a forest. At first, I imagined Kodama as a sprout,
but later settled on the idea of an acorn who has a very big cap.

Kodama was modeled and rigged in Autodesk Maya. Trees and flowers were made by my teammates.

The magic seeds would be protected by a large flower that would only open once you collected all the crystals in the stage.

Mother tells Kodama that she is ill, and that the only cure is to collect the Magic Seeds.

Kodama embarks on a journey to save Mother and everything he loves!
